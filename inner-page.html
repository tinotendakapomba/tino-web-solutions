Tinotenda Kapomba

Hello! I'm Tinotenda Kapomba, a 20-year-old web designer born in Zimbabwe. From a young age, I've harbored a passionate interest in technology, which has shaped my journey into the world of web development.

I am currently pursuing a degree in Software Engineering at Bindura University. Over the past two years, I've dedicated myself to designing and building websites, developing a strong foundation in both front-end and back-end frameworks, including Django, Angular, React, and Node.js. I pride myself on my ability to create fully responsive websites that enhance user experience.

I understand that the learning journey is ongoing, and I am committed to improving my skills every day. In addition to web design, I also create 3D games using the Unreal Engine, utilizing C++ as my primary programming language.
